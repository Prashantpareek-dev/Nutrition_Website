Our Story

About Our Nutrition & Diet Consultancy

A professional nutrition consultancy committed to making healthy eating accessible, practical, and truly personalized for every individual.

Who We Are

We Are SS Herbal India

We are a professional nutrition consultancy focused on structured and practical diet solutions. Our work is built on one belief: that good nutrition should be simple, accessible, and tailored to the real person behind the goal.

We work with individuals across all walks of life—from busy professionals to students, homemakers to entrepreneurs—helping them build healthy eating habits that genuinely last. Every consultation we deliver is grounded in nutrition knowledge and human understanding.

Our Direction

Mission & Vision

What we stand for and where we're headed—the principles that guide every consultation we deliver.

Our Mission

To simplify nutrition and make personalized diet consultation accessible for everyone. We believe that understanding what to eat shouldn't require a degree—it should be clear, practical, and achievable.

Our Vision

To help individuals build long-term healthy eating habits through structured diet planning and compassionate guidance. A world where every person has the knowledge and support to nourish themselves well.

Client-Centric Model

Your Lifestyle, Your Diet Plan

Every diet plan is built around your lifestyle—not the other way around. We start with understanding who you are, how you live, and what you enjoy eating before making a single recommendation.

This means we take time to learn your work schedule, cooking habits, family dynamics, cultural food preferences, and personal goals. Only then do we build a plan that feels natural and sustainable.
