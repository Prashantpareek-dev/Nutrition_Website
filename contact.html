FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our diet consultation services and process.

What happens during an initial diet consultation?

In your first session, we'll discuss your current diet habits, lifestyle, health goals, food preferences, and daily routine. This helps us build a clear picture to create your personalized diet plan.

How long is a consultation session?

Initial consultations typically last 45–60 minutes. Follow-up sessions are usually 20–30 minutes. All sessions are conducted online via video call.

Is the consultation available online?

Yes, absolutely. All our consultations are conducted online via video call, making them accessible from any location across India and internationally.

How soon will I receive my personalized diet plan?

Your personalized diet plan is typically shared within 24–48 hours after your initial consultation, once we've had time to properly customize it based on your needs.

Is this a replacement for medical treatment?

No. Our services are advisory and consultation-based only. We provide nutrition guidance and diet planning, but we are not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis or treatment. Always consult a qualified doctor for medical conditions.

Can I reschedule my consultation?

Yes. Rescheduling is allowed with prior notice. Please notify us at least a few hours before your scheduled session to reschedule without issue. Refer to our Refund & Cancellation Policy for details.
